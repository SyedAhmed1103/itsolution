Michel jhon is a leading e-commerce retailer specializing in high-quality home decor products. With a growing customer base, they faced challenges in optimizing their website for better user experience and increasing sales conversions

Service is designed to provide you with tailored solutions that improve user engagement, drive conversions, and optimize your digital experiences. Whether it’s building a responsive website or crafting an intuitive app, our solutions are built with your audience in mind.

The challenge

Despite a strong brand presence, Michel's website had a high bounce rate, slow load times, and lacked mobile optimization. Users were struggling to find products quickly, and the checkout process was complicated, leading to cart abandonment

The goal

The goal was to create a more engaging and user-friendly e-commerce website with faster load times, responsive design, and a simplified shopping experience. The client aimed to improve user retention, reduce bounce rates, and ultimately increase sales conversions

Solution

We conducted user research to identify pain points and implemented a mobile-first design approach. Our team revamped the product search and navigation to make it more intuitive, optimized the checkout process, and integrated performance-enhancing features like image compression for faster load times
